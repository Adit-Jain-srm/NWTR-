# NWTR — Lessons & Patterns

## Business Model
- The yield math only closes at 70-80% deposit-to-value ratio with blended instruments
- Position as "investment deposit" not "security deposit" to avoid MTA 2-month cap
- SEBI CIS classification is existential risk — must resolve pre-launch

## Design
- "Quiet Luxury" motion: 400-600ms deliberate pacing signals premium for HNI
- Specificity over scale in trust signals ("1 in 6 Powai homes" > "5M users")
- Truva.in is the closest Indian design benchmark for premium proptech

## Technical
- Data localization mandatory for financial data (RBI requirement)
- Azure India regions required for NBFC compliance
- eKYC/VKYC/CKYC stack must be tiered by transaction value
