## Methodology Notes

### Source Selection Criteria

1. **Recency**: Preference for 2024-2025 data; foundational academic works accepted regardless of age
2. **Authority**: Government sources, regulated entity publications, and peer-reviewed research prioritized
3. **Relevance**: Each source must directly support at least one claim in the NWTR documentation suite
4. **Accessibility**: Publicly available sources preferred; proprietary reports noted where essential

### Citation Protocol

- Regulatory references cite specific circular/notification numbers for legal traceability
- Financial data sources cite the specific dataset/table within larger publications
- Academic papers follow standard APA citation conventions
- URLs point to landing pages rather than deep links (which break frequently)
